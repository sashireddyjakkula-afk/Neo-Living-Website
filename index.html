The idea

Most homes are built to be looked at. We build homes to be felt — alive with light, greenery, texture and sound.

Neo Living began with a simple observation: most homes ask almost nothing of your senses. Flat light, still air, hard surfaces, not a leaf in sight. They can be perfectly efficient and still leave you feeling nothing. We think that's a failure of design, not of ambition.

So we're starting differently. Our answer isn't a retreat to rustic cabins or an all-natural aesthetic — it's clean, modern architecture, infused with nature. Before a single project, we're setting down what we stand for: how a Neo Living home should feel to move through, and how it keeps you close to the natural world, day after day.

A great home isn't just seen. It's felt — in the light, the air, and everything you touch.

What guides us

Three commitments, before anything is built.

N°01

Rooted in place

Design begins with the land — its light, water, slope and trees — not with a floor plan stamped onto a site. We build around what's already there.

N°02

Alive to the senses

Daylight, greenery, natural materials, water and sound — every space is composed to engage sight, touch, sound and scent, not simply to be looked at.

N°03

Made for wellbeing

Homes that keep the natural world within reach — long views, gardens, fresh air and quiet — because being close to nature is what makes a place feel good to live in.
